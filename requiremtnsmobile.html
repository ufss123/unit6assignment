Job Requirements and Specifications

At Hot Beans Web, we believe that our success is driven by the talent, creativity, and dedication of our team. We are currently seeking dynamic individuals who are passionate about web development and eager to contribute to innovative projects. Below are the key job requirements and specifications for individuals looking to join our growing team.

Key Responsibilities

As a member of the Hot Beans Web team, your responsibilities will include:

  • Designing and developing responsive websites and web applications.
  • Collaborating with designers, developers, and stakeholders to implement innovative web solutions.
  • Writing clean, scalable, and efficient code following best practices.
  • Ensuring websites and applications are optimized for speed and usability.
  • Conducting testing and debugging to ensure the functionality and performance of web applications.
  • Staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends, technologies, and frameworks.

Job Requirements

To be successful in this role, candidates must meet the following qualifications and skills:

  • Proven experience in web development (at least 2 years preferred).
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern JavaScript frameworks (e.g., React, Angular, Vue.js).
  • Familiarity with server-side programming languages such as PHP, Node.js, or Ruby.
  • Experience with version control systems, such as Git.
  • Strong understanding of web performance optimization techniques.
  • Experience with database management (e.g., MySQL, MongoDB).
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Ability to work independently as well as part of a collaborative team.
  • Strong communication skills to interact with both technical and non-technical team members.

Preferred Qualifications

In addition to the basic requirements, candidates with the following skills and experience will be given priority consideration:

  • Experience with cloud services (e.g., AWS, Azure).
  • Familiarity with Agile development methodologies.
  • Experience in UX/UI design or working closely with design teams.
  • Knowledge of mobile app development and cross-platform frameworks.
